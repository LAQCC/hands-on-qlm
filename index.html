Welcome to the Atos Quantum Learning Machine

The Quantum Learning Machine, or QLM, is the quantum program appliance of Atos. This machine is a complete environment designed for quantum software developers, engineers and researchers. It is dedicated to the development of quantum software, training and experimentation. The Atos Quantum Learning Machine provides different environments:

  • Programming
  • Optimizing
  • Simulating
